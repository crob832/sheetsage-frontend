FAQ

Questions that matter

Is it safe to run Fix All?

Yes. SheetSage creates a snapshot first so you always have rollback.

Do you store my spreadsheet data?

Spreadsheet content is designed to stay inside Google Sheets. Subscription checks (if you upgrade) send only an anonymous client ID.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime. Free features remain available.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free is for discovering risk and fixing within limits. Pro is for batch fixing (Fix All) and unlimited volume.
