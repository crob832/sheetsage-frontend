FAQs

Is it safe to run Fix All?

Yes. SheetSage creates a snapshot first so you always have rollback.

Do you store my spreadsheet data?

SheetSage is designed to keep spreadsheet content inside Sheets. Licensing checks send only an anonymous client ID.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime. Free features remain available.

What’s the difference between Free and Pro?

Free = discover and fix within limits. Pro = batch fixes (Fix All) + unlimited volume + no caps.
